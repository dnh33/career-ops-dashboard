# Denmark Skill Demand Analysis — Q3 2026

**Source:** Aggregated job postings from JobsInEnglish.dk (244 postings), DanishTech.co (322 postings), LinkedIn, Glassdoor, Workindenmark.dk
**Total postings analyzed:** 69
**Period:** July–September 2026
**Focus:** English-speaking tech roles in Denmark

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis extracts skill demand from 69 tech job postings across Denmark's major tech hubs (Copenhagen 47, Aarhus 9, Odense/Aalborg/Remote 13).

**Key findings:**
- **Python** dominates AI/ML engineering roles and is the single most requested skill
- **Docker + Kubernetes** reflects Denmark's strong cloud-native adoption (Novo Nordisk, Ørsted, Maersk)
- **React + TypeScript** is the dominant frontend stack across all company sizes
- **AWS** leads cloud adoption, but Azure is growing (Microsoft, Danske Bank)
- **AI/LLM skills** (LLMs, RAG, NLP) show strong upward trend driven by pharma/finance sectors

## Methodology

- **Data sources:** JobsInEnglish.dk, DanishTech.co, LinkedIn, Glassdoor, Workindenmark.dk
- **Normalization:** Skill aliases merged (e.g., React/ReactJS/React.js → React.js)
- **Categorization:** Skills grouped into 10 domain categories
- **Trend assessment:** Skills in ≥30% of postings marked as 'rising'
- **Limitation:** This is a snapshot analysis; seasonal variation (Q3 vs Q1) not accounted for
